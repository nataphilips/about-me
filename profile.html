About Me

My name is Natalia Filippova and I am from Moscow.

In 2011 I graduated from National Research University of Electronic Technology (diploma of a Specialist in Marketing) and started working in an advertising agency. I've been working there for 6 years. Then I moved out from Moscow and started traveling, and this was the time I got inspired from my programmer friends to change my profession to tech field.

I've been studying systematically since March 2019, before that I just did a few videos and small challenges.By this time I got through Front End course on Udemy, several katas and some personal exercise projects. During these month I focused mostly on React as this framework is in high demand and is actually very convenient. Even though I studied a lot by myself, I'm missing working in a team or a mentor's feedback. A friend told me about this bootcamp, and this is why I started applying to participate.

Superhero Fight (React)

Provides an opportunity to check which superhero or supervillain is stronger (with a fair share of luck for each :)).

Key statistics are: intelligence, strength and speed. Each supercharacter has a superpower which modifies values of these statistics of himself/herself or his/her opponent.

Pick a character by clicking on his/her picture and press Fight button. That's it! You'll see average damage dealt by each character and % of time that they dodged from rival's attacks. Reset the fighting pair of characters by clicking on the portraits showing who is chosen, or just by clicking on Reset button.
